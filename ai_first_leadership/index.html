Risks, Ethics & Anti-Abuse Guidance

Over-Reliance & Deskilling

Teams may lose critical thinking skills if they defer too much to AI.

Mitigation: Maintain "AI-free" decision practices. Require human validation for high-stakes choices. Train critical evaluation.

Surveillance Creep

AI tools can enable invasive monitoring of employee work.

Mitigation: Transparent policies. AI for augmentation, not surveillance. Team input on tool selection.

Bias Amplification

AI can encode and scale biases in hiring, performance, and decisions.

Mitigation: Regular bias audits. Diverse review of AI recommendations. Human override requirements.

AI Anxiety & Exclusion

Some team members may feel threatened or left behind.

Mitigation: Psychological safety first. Inclusive learning. Clear communication that AI augments, doesn't replace.

Privacy & Data Risks

Sensitive information shared with AI systems may be exposed.

Mitigation: Clear data policies. Approved tool lists. Training on what not to share.
