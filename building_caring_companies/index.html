Care Culture Pitfalls

Performative Care

Saying the right things without backing them up. Yoga classes while overworking people.

Mitigation: Match actions to words. Ask employees if care feels real.

Care as Control

Using "family" language to manipulate. "We're a family" often means "we expect sacrifice."

Mitigation: Respect boundaries. Don't use care language to guilt.

Selective Care

Caring about some people but not others. Favoritism disguised as culture.

Mitigation: Consistent treatment across roles, tenures, identities.

Care Without Accountability

Being so "nice" that problems aren't addressed. Care includes honest feedback.

Mitigation: Caring includes holding standards. Kindness ‚â† avoidance.
