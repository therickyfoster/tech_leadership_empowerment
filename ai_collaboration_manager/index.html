Prompt Library for Managers

Ready-to-use prompts for common management tasks. Customize for your context.

1:1 Preparation

- Suggesting 3 check-in questions
- Identifying potential topics to discuss
- Drafting a question about their career growth

Difficult Conversation Prep

I need to have a conversation with [name] about [issue]. I want to be direct but supportive. Help me:
- Frame the issue clearly
- Anticipate their perspective
- Plan specific, actionable next steps
- Identify how to show support while being clear

- Key criteria to consider

- Questions to ask stakeholders

Risks & Anti-Patterns

AI-Generated Inauthenticity

Communications feel robotic. Team members notice AI-generated patterns. Trust erodes.

Mitigation: AI drafts, you personalize. Add your voice, specific references, genuine emotion.

Over-Delegation to AI

Letting AI make decisions that require human judgment. Missing context AI can't see.

Mitigation: AI informs, humans decide. Always add context AI doesn't have.

Privacy Violations

Sharing sensitive employee information with AI systems inappropriately.

Mitigation: Know your company's AI data policies. Anonymize when possible. Don't share without consent.

Deskilling

Losing fundamental management skills by over-relying on AI assistance.

Mitigation: Use AI to enhance, not replace, your skills. Practice without AI periodically.
